## Attributions

- **Tatoeba** — sentence pairs under CC-BY 2.0 FR. © Tatoeba contributors,
  https://tatoeba.org . Used for the M1 tokenizer bootstrap.
- **KFTT (Kyoto Free Translation Task)** — CC-BY-SA 3.0, by Graham Neubig,
  http://www.phontron.com/kftt/ . The published silver annotations, demo data, and
  the M6/M7 teacher data are all derived from KFTT Japanese sentences, so they are
  released **CC-BY-SA 3.0** (share-alike). Attribute KFTT + this project.
- **JESC (Japanese-English Subtitle Corpus)** — CC-BY-SA 4.0. Used only to build
  the (unpublished) M2 corpus.
- **JParaCrawl** — research-use, web-crawl-derived; **not redistributed here**. It

  pipeline regenerates it from source.
- **MeCab** (BSD) + **UniDic** (GPL/LGPL/BSD tri-license) — used offline under
  `tools/annotate/` for deterministic segmentation/ruby/POS labels. Redistributable.

## Model-generated content

English translations and glosses in the M6 distillation data and the M7 silver
annotations were generated by **OpenAI models** (gpt-5.6 family). Outputs are used
per OpenAI's terms (the user owns the outputs); flagged here for transparency, as
is standard for model-distilled datasets.

## Model weights

Weights (translation M3, annotation M8, tokenizers) are derived artifacts trained
on a JParaCrawl-heavy corpus. Publishing weights does not redistribute the corpus;
provenance is documented in `docs/MODEL_CARDS.md`.
